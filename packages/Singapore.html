Overview

The charm of Singapore reflects through its unique culture, lush greenery, and architectural wonders. You can plan your International tour at this dreamy destination to get the best holiday experience. We have a well-designed 5 days Singapore tour package that will give you a new adventure every day. With this tour package, you can explore various attractions throughout your journey.

This tour package will take you on a ride to cover the prime attractions of Singapore, such as Sentosa Island, Singapore Zoo, Universal Studio, and many more. There is so much to do and see when you are in Singapore, so peruse this itinerary for more details. Book this 4 nights 5 days Singapore tour package to have a luxurious and comfortable experience that leaves indelible marks on your heart.

Note: It is a suggested itinerary of Singapore for 4 nights and 5 days that displays what could be possible. We modify the tour package as per your need and budget to give you a satisfactory travel experience.

Highlights

  • Witness cultural programs and enjoy a night safari at Singapore Zoo.
  • Take a fascinating tour of Sentosa Island.
  • Explore theme zones at Universal Studios.
  • Get a refreshing break by visiting the Garden by the Bay With Marina Bay Sand.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arriving in Singapore

This Singapore tour commences as you arrive at Singapore airport. After arriving, you will clear your immigration and get a transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Once you reach the hotel, you will complete the check-in process and move towards your room. Later, you will rest for a while before moving out for the Singapore Zoo.

Then, you will take an excursion to Night Safari and witness the wide range of animals. After that, you will have the opportunity to see the cultural performances, including tribal dances, fire-eating, blowpipe demonstrations. Next, you will have to see the Creature of the Night Show and get a unique experience on the first day of your 5 Days Singapore tour. Later, you will return to your hotel to enjoy your overnight stay on a comfortable bed in your hotel.

Day 2: Sightseeing Tour of Sentosa Island

Start the day with a healthy breakfast in your hotel and enjoy the leisure activity. Then, you will take a Sentosa Twilight Tour that covers a one-way cable car ride, admission ticket, Madame Tussauds 5 in 1, Lunge, and Skyride in the afternoon. Moreover, you will have the opportunity to explore many attractions like the SEA Aquarium, Sentosa Beaches, Adventure Cove Waterpark, etc.

Most of the attractions are located on Resorts World Sentosa and Sentosa Beachfront. After filling your heart with fun, thrill, and everlasting memories, return to your hotel for an overnight stay on a comfortable bed.

Day 3: Visit Universal Studio

Rise and shine in the morning and welcome the day with a refreshing breakfast in the hotel. After finishing your breakfast, you will be taken to Universal Studios. It is one of the most popular attractions in Singapore. You will explore various theme zones in this universal studio on the third day of your Singapore tour. This studio features Hollywood, New York, Sci-Fi City, Ancient Egypt, The Lost World, Far Far Away, and Madagascar zones.

This day gives you the best outlook on the adventure, from getting an adrenaline pump to getting a new experience with each zone. Later, you will get back to your hotel and hop on your bed for a relaxing overnight stay.

Day 4: Garden by The Bay with Marina Bay Sand Sky Park

Wake up early in the morning with a healthy breakfast and be ready to fill your heart with double fun. Today, you will take a fascinating Garden By The Bay tour with Marina Bay Sands Sky Park. Firstly, you will explore Garden by the Bay; a nature park spread to 101 hectares. Next, you will admire the supertrees-like structure that dominates the garden's landscape.

The height of this structure range from 25 meters to 50 meters. Next, you will explore The Flower Dome & Cloud Forest; these are two conservatories. These vertical gardens of Singapore perform various functions, including planting, shading, and working as environmental engines. After exploring this attraction, you will get back to your hotel room to have a sound sleep on your comfortable bed.

Day 5: Departure from Singapore

You will welcome the morning vibes with a healthy breakfast on this day. After finishing your breakfast, you will have free time until the tour representative reaches the hotel to give you a transfer to the airport. Our representative will make all arrangements in advance to provide you with ease and a comfortable journey.
