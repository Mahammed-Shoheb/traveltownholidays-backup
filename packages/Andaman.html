Highlights

  • Excursion to Rajiv Gandhi Water Sports Complex in Port Blair
  • Attend the Light and Sound Show at Cellular Jail
  • Ferry from Port Blair to Havelock Island
  • A day trip to Blue Flag Coveted Radhanagar Beach
  • Watersports at Havelock beaches like Laxmanpur, Elephant Beach
  • Day out at Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep)
  • Rejuvenation at Port Blair's top beach, Corbyn's Cove

Itinerary

Day 1: Port Blair: Arrival, Sightseeing Overnight Stay

Arrive at the Port Blair Airport, Veer Savarkar Airport; an IHPL representative will meet you and assist you to your hotel. The capital city of Andaman Islands, Port Blair, is famous for its pristine natural beauty and serene environment. The city offers fabulous views of coconut and palm trees, azure blue water and gleaming sand. It is one of the major tourist places in India.

As you reach the hotel, complete the check-in formalities and rest for a while. Your 6 nights and 7 days Andaman itinerary will start in the daytime. You can spend time at the Water Sports Complex (closed on Wednesday) so plan your trip accordingly. Here, options to indulge in watersports activities are endless, from banana boat ride to glass-bottom ride, from jet ski to speed boat ride.

We will escort you to the Cellular Jail as the evening hits to attend the Light and Sound Show. Watch the heroic saga of the Indian freedom struggle through poignant music and light show. The show will make your family trip to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands a memorable one.

After a relaxing evening, we will escort you to the hotel, where you will have your dinner doze off!

Note: Our Andaman 7 day itinerary excludes the Light and Sound Show ticket at Cellular Jail. Moreover, you have to pay additional charges if you want to enjoy watersports at the Water Sports Complex.

Day 2: Havelock Island: Arrival by Ferry, Sightseeing, and Overnight Stay

This morning, wake up to the drool-worthy breakfast, complete the check-out formalities, and travel to Havelock Island. It is a must-visit place on a trip to Andaman. You will reach here by ferry. Here the prismatic beaches have shimmering blue water, and the shoreline is flanked by the palm trees. Havelock Island is one of the best places in Andaman to watch the underwater coral colonies. Upon your arrival at Havelock Island, check in at the hotel and relax for some time.

After a few hours, visit the Blue Flag coveted Radhanagar Beach. Such is the allure of this beach that it has been honored as 'Asia's best beach by the famous 'Times Magazine.' The water quality is similar to the Maldives beaches. Furthermore, the visibility during snorkeling and scuba diving is awesome. Imagine yourself surrounded by a peaceful environment where white sands are your holiday playground. From snorkeling to swimming and other watersports are in abundance at Radhanagar Beach.

Distance from Port Blair to Havelock Island: 70 kilometers (approx.)

Travel Time: 3 hours 15 minutes (approx.)

Note: Your Andaman itinerary for 6 days here on day 2 does not include the watersports cost at Radhanagar Beach. One must have to pay the additional cost to indulge in watersports.

Day 3: Havelock Island: Full Day Sightseeing

Begin your day with a scrumptious breakfast at your resort/hotel, and then lace up your sneakers to discover the top places to visit on Havelock Island. You would be amazed to know that Havelock Island (now Swaraj Dweep) has one of the best beaches in Andaman. Moreover, the sand here is sugary white, and the marvelous reef and rapturous swimming elephants could be seen because Havelock Island has lush green tropical vegetation.

For a wholesome experience at the beaches, cover as much as the beaches other than Randhanagar on day 3. For instance, Elephant Beach is a lively beach for swimming and boating. Vijaynagar Beach has a relaxing aura, and the palm trees grace the setting. Also, the Govindnagar Beach has swanky resorts and beach restaurants to nosh on sea food. Enjoy snorkeling or spend the day lying on the beach and relaxing. The hard coral reef here starts close to shore at a depth of about 1 meter and has plenty of marine life.

Once you are done with the Havelock Island sightseeing, return to the hotel/resort for overnight sleep. And yes, your dinner is also included!

Note: We have not included any water sport activity cost on day 3 also. Hence, you need to pay an additional amount to enjoy the same. Moreover, you can customize the package where we will quote you the price with the watersports cost.

Day 4: Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep) Arrival, Sightseeing, and Overnight Stay

We urge you to wake up early and nosh on your breakfast at the hotel. Afterward, complete the check-out formalities and board a jetty from the dock to Neil Island (now Shaheed Dweep). Upon arrival, get transferred to the hotel by our representative and do the check-in formalities. Then, after a few minutes or an hour of relaxing, embark on sightseeing at the beaches like Laxmanpur Beach and Bharatpur Beach for scuba diving and snorkeling to uncover the underwater marine world.

Later, return to the hotel for an overnight stay after a delicious dinner.

Distance from Havelock Island to Neil Island: 40 kilometers (approx.)

Travel Time: 2 hours (approx.)

Note: No activities charges are included on day 4 also! Therefore, you need to pay additional charges if you want to opt for water sports at Neil Island's beaches.

Day 5: Port Blair: Back to the Capital Town via Ferry

Wake up to a tooth-some breakfast at the hotel and complete the check-out formalities. You will be transferred to the jetty by our representative, where you will board the ferry to Port Blair. Once you reach the capital, you will be escorted to the hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure.

Distance from Neil Island to Port Blair: 54 kilometers (approx.)

Travel Time: 3 hours (approx.)

Day 6: Port Blair: City Tour and Corbyn's Cove Beach

Start the day with an appetizing breakfast, start for a tour of places in and around Port Blair. We suggest you start your sightseeing early so that you can cover most of the attractions in Port Blair like Chatham Saw Mill, Forest Museum, Fisheries Museum, Anthropological Museum, Naval Marine Museum. After lunch hours, we will escort you to Corbyn's Cove beach, which is a sandy palm-fringed beach. It is the only beach in Port Blair that is ideal for relaxing and unwinding. Proceed for local shopping.

Note: This 6 night 7 days Andaman itinerary does not include any activity cost and entry ticket on day 6. However, if you customize the package, you can enjoy this benefit.

Day 7: Port Blair: Farewell and Goodbye

This will be your departure day on a 6 nights and 7 days Andaman tour with Neil Island. Hence, wake up early to a healthy meal, pack your belongings, do the checkout formalities, and meet our representative outside the hotel. He will escort you to the Veer Savarkar International Airport, where you will board your flight back home.
