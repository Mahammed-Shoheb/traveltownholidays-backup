Uttarakhand Family Tour

Overview

Uttarakhand is renowned as the “Land of Gods” and is considered the paradise on the earth for its scenic beauty. With our meticulously crafted 7 Nights/ 8 Days Uttarakhand family tour, you will get a chance to explore the tourist places such as Auli, Nainital, Corbett, Mukteshwar, Kausani, and many more.

During the tour, families can indulge in several activities such as boating, cable car ride, skiing, tea plantation tour, enjoy elephant rides, and jeep safari at Jim Corbett National Park. The tour package has some inclusions such as delicious meals, cozy accommodations, swift transfers, and a sightseeing tour. You can also customize the itinerary as per your requirements and budget.

Note: This is just a suggested itinerary indicative of what could be possible. We tailor holidays for your specific needs. Contact us if you want modifications so that we could tailor a holiday to suit your need for an unforgettable India tour.

Highlights

  • Darshan at the Mukteshwar temple
  • Delight the boat ride in Nainital Lake
  • Enjoy skiing and cable car ride at Auli
  • Experience the jeep safari at Jim Corbett
  • Visit famous Tal - Bhimtal, Sattal and naukuchiatal
  • Visit to anashkati ashram and tea estate, Kausani
  • Trek up to the exotic valley of flowers from Joshimath

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrval at Haridwar - Auli

In the morning leave from your hotel in Dehradun/Haridwar to reach Auli. Auli is the most popular skiing destination of Uttarakhand state. The slopes here provide enough thrill to professional skier and novice as well.

On the way to Auli enjoy beautiful landscapes and scenic beauty. On the way to Auli enjoy the breathtaking panoramic view of the lofty peaks of Himalayas. On reaching Auli, check-in at one of our enlisted resorts. Spend the rest of the day at leisure. In the evening enjoy Sunset on Nanda Devi. Overnight stay at the resort.

Day 2: Auli -Joshimath (temple tour)

After breakfast, head straight to Joshimath for local sightseeing in and around the city. On day 02 the major hotspots to visit in Joshimath include the Narsimha Temple, Shankracharya cave, Tapovan Temple and the Hot Sulphur Water Springs. Later in the day you may trek up to the exotic valley of flowers from Joshimath. Return to the resort in Auli for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Auli - Kausani

In the morning wake up early and after breakfast, proceed to Kausani by road. Kausani was described as 'Mini-Switzerland of India' by Mahatma Gandhi. On reaching Kausani check-in at one of our listed hotels. Later in the day embark on a sightseeing tour of the city. The major tourist hotspots to visit include the Anashkati Ashram Laxmi Ashram, Sumitranandan Pant Gallery and the Kausani tea estate. Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 4: Kausani- Mukteshwar

Post breakfast, head straight to Mukteshwar from Kausani. Mukteshwar is a sacred shrine that holds great importance amongst the people of Uttrakhand. The Shivling placed in the temple is surrounded by idols of Parvati, Hanuman, Nandi, Brahma and Vishnu. The Mukteshwar temple is situated at an astounding elevation of 2312 meters above the sea level.

En route to Mukteshwar while crossing Almora halt to buy some Baal-Mithai, the local sweet delicacy. On the drive to Mukteshwar also visit Bhowali-the biggest fruit market in Uttarakhand and Ramgarh, famous as the ‘fruit bowl of Kumaon’. On reaching Mukteshwar get accommodated at one of our listed hotels.

Enjoy Darshan at the Mukteshwar temple. Relish dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 5: Mukteshwar - Bhimtal- Nainital

Post breakfast, commence your journey to Bhimtal. On the way to the Bhimtal stop midway to visit Ghorakhal- for the famous army school, Naukuchiatal and Sattal. After arriving Nainital check-in at your hotel. Spend the rest of the day at rest. Enjoy dinner and overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 6: Nainital

Post breakfast, move out for a full day embark on a sightseeing tour of Nainital.

The major tourist attractions include the Khurpatal Lake, Waterfalls, Hanuman Mandir, Observatory etc. Also enjoy the cable car ride to Tiffin Top and boat ride in Naini Lake. In the evening enjoy shopping at Mall Road. Enjoy an overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 7: Nainital - Corbett

On the 7th day of the tour after breakfast, move out for a full day excursion to the World Famous Tiger Reserve- The Corbett National Park. The park offers you to visit a wide variety of species like Tigers, leopard and elephants. Experience an adventurous jeep safari and explore the rich flora and fauna of the park. Enjoy bonfire and dinner at the hotel in the evening. Night stay at the hotel.

Day 8: Corbett - Haridwar - Dehradun

In the morning after breakfast, check out of the hotel and leave for your journey back to Haridwar/Dehradun for the onward destination.
