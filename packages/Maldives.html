Budget Trip to Maldives

*Price on request

Overview

Browse this 4 nights and 5 days budget trip to Maldives itinerary we have crafted to give you the best experiences in Maldives capital, Malé. The capital town is one of the inhabited islands in the Indian Ocean and boasts whimsical vibes. Indian Holiday budget-friendly Maldives trip could be booked from India’s metro towns like Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Ahmedabad.

This package to the Maldives from India is crafted in a way so that you will have ample time to adhere to each holiday element in the Maldives, including cultural sightseeing, heritage sightseeing, local experience, watersports fun. This trip's starting and ending point will be Malé, and once you arrive at the resort, all the days will be at your leisure.

We have included 3-star accommodation in this package with speedboat pickup/drop meals. The accommodation will be in 3 variants - sea view room, beachfront, standard, superior. Feel free to customize your trip if you want. Meanwhile, take a look at this suggested budget Maldives trip itinerary!

Important Note: Any activity which is not mentioned in this package as inclusion would be treated as exclusion with additional cost.

Highlights

  • Take a dig into Maldivan culture and history
  • Stroll around the pristine beaches
  • Go for island hopping on a yacht or speedboat
  • Explore the length and breath of Malé by foot within 45 minutes (approx.)
  • Sunset photography in pink-hued backdrops

Itinerary

Day 1: Maldives: Arrival at Malé International Airport and Hotel Transfer Via Speedboat

After landing at Velana International Airport in Malé, our representative would welcome you with warm greetings. After giving your luggage, followed by loading in the speedboat, enjoy the speedboat ride for a few minutes to get transferred to your accommodation.

Once you reach, complete the check-in formalities at your resort. Put your backpack for a while and relax in your room. If you want, you can ask the resort staff to give you a mini-tour of the property. You can hang out outside your property in the afternoon or evening and explore the historical attractions in Malé to get a sneak peek into the Maldivian culture.

Come back to your hotel to enjoy an overnight stay followed by a delicious meal.

Optional: Historical sightseeing is optional and is just an example of what you could explore on your arrival day at Malé. However, you can do whatever you want in Malé at your own expense.

Day 2: Malé: Day Reserved For Watersports and Island Hopping

Treat your tastebuds to the flavorsome breakfast, and then head out to spend the full day at the beaches. Whether you are with your soulmate or family or friends, or even solo, fun frolic moments are guaranteed because the sand is pristine at the beaches of Malé.

You can cruise the islands surrounding the Malé for a change on a speedboat. You will have the full day at your leisure. Make sure you wake up early and start your day by plunging into the blue waters for the swim, followed by snorkeling, scuba diving, banana boat ride, etc.

Photograph yourself in the pink and orange-hued backdrops of Malé landscape either at the beaches or on the island hopping expedition.

As day 2 of your budget trip to Maldives ends, return to the hotel for savoring the toothsome dinner and overnight sleep.

Optional: Beach hopping, island hopping is just a suggestion. You can spend your time at your ease by doing various other activities in and around Malé at your own expense.

Day 3: Malé: Enjoy the Day At Your Convenience

Wake up to the hearty meal at your preferred waking time. Afterward, lace up your boots because day 3 is again reserved for enjoying at your own convenience. Make it count by discovering something unique other than the beaches and watersports.

Marvel the elegance of dozens of the Mosques in the Malé. Learn about the historical treasures of Maldives at the Museums. Inhale fresh air amidst the lush greenery at the Sultan Park (Rasrani Bageecha), and end your evening with a shopping excursion to the local markets to buy Maldivan handicrafts and stuff.

Optional: All the activities you will be performing at your own expense. Only breakfast, dinner, and stay are included on day 3.

Day 4: Malé: Another Day to Get Pampered With Relaxation

Start your morning with an appetizing breakfast and pick the opportunity to enjoy day 4 at your own preferences. This will be the second last day of a 4 nights and 5 days budget trip to the Maldives so explore the island on foot because the whole of Malé city could be easily covered within the 45 minutes (approx.) of foot expedition.

In the afternoon or evening, head to the beach till you watch the last sunset on your Maldives trip from India.

After completing day 4 sightseeing, return to your hotel for an overnight stay after dinner.

Optional: You are free to do anything. If a foot journey is not your cup of tea, taxis are very cheap in the Malé to explore.

Day 5: Malé International Airport: Transfer from Resort to Fly Back Home

Bow down your head at the sapphire blue waters of the last time all around you because, after a lip-smacking breakfast and check-out procedure, you will be escorted to the Velana International Airport to fly back to your home with a bag full of Maldives holiday memories.
