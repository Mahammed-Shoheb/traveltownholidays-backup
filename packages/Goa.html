Goa Honeymoon Package

Overview

Goa is undoubtedly the top honeymoon destination for couples from all over the world. Its picturesque beaches with their silky golden sands and magnificent views provide the best setting for couples who are looking to have a good time on their Goa Honeymoon Tour. The beaches of Goa are categorized as North Goa and South Goa beaches. However, they are quite different from each other. While the beaches in North Goa including Vagator Beach, Baga Beach and Anjuna Beach, among others, are famous for hosting the most electrifying parties in town, those in South Goa like Butterfly Beach and Majorda Beach are quiet and great for those who are looking to have a relaxing day at the beach. However, regardless of whether you spend time in North Goa or South Goa, you will find romance, excitement, and adventure in plenty. We offer the Goa honeymoon package which provides an opportunity to explore the best of the Goa with your spouse and give you a memorable trip.

Note: This is just a suggested itinerary indicative of what could be possible. We tailor holidays for your specific needs. Contact us if you want modifications so that we could tailor a holiday to suit your need for an unforgettable India tour.

Highlights

  • Guided trip to Fort Aguada and Old Goa churches
  • Excursion to Mangeshi Temple, Santa Durga Temple, and Colva Beach
  • Boat cruise on the Mandovi River
  • Indulge in water sports- snorkeling, scuba diving, and others

Itinerary

Day 1: Goa (Arrival)

Upon your arrival at the Goa airport, get in touch with our representative who will assist you with a smooth transfer to the beach resort. Take rest for a while and then get ready for the outing. Your honeymoon trip to Goa starts with a boat cruise on the Mandovi River in the evening. Experience the thrill and luxury of traveling on-board with your spouse. While cruising in the river you will enjoy the DJ sessions, and Goan folk dances with a backdrop of clear blue sea and setting sun. This is followed by your return to the hotel for dinner and an overnight stay.

Day 2: North Goa

On the 2nd day of your Goa honeymoon package, you will go on an excursion to North Goa. Enjoy your breakfast and then head towards North Goa. There are a few beaches in North Goa which are very popular among tourists including Baga Beach, Dona Paula, Calangute, and Miramar. You can stroll around these beaches and enjoy the special time with your significant other. You can also enjoy water sports activities like snorkeling, scuba diving, jet-skiing, and banana boat ride in Calangute. Other places where you can have a great time on Goa honeymoon are Fort Aguada and the ancient churches in Old Goa. The beautiful churches in Goa are a perfect amalgamation of Indian and Portuguese architecture. Return to the resort in the evening and have a sumptuous dinner. Overnight stay at the hotel in Goa.

Day 3: South Goa

Start the day with a delicious breakfast and make your way towards the beaches of South Goa. Some of the popular attractions of the Goa tour package for a couple includes the Santa Durga Temple, Colva Beach, and Mangeshi Temple, along with a few other places. Take a long walk on the golden sand of Colva Beach on a moony evening with the love of your life. Colva Beach is one of the famous tourist destinations among honeymooners. One can also enjoy the nightlife in Goa and relish the fascinating sightseeing of the dazzling places. One can also take part in water sports activities. Return to the resort for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Goa

After enjoying your breakfast in the morning, complete your check-out formalities at the hotel. With this, your Goa honeymoon package came to an end. Ride to the airport to board the flight.
